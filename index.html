FAQ

Questions people ask before upgrading their network

Do you only install UniFi equipment?

UniFi is the primary platform because it provides a strong balance of performance, visibility, scalability, and long-term value. Existing systems can also be evaluated and repaired where practical.

Can you improve WiFi in a detached garage, barn, or shop?

Yes. Depending on the distance and property layout, the best option may be outdoor access points, wireless bridges, buried fiber, or a combination of those technologies.

Do you work with both homes and businesses?

Yes. Services are available for homes, small businesses, offices, studios, shops, rural properties, and multi-building locations.

Can you replace the router provided by my internet company?

Often, yes. The final design depends on your provider and service type, but UniFi gateways and access points can usually provide far better coverage and control.
