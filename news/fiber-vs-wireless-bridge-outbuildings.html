Fiber or Wireless Bridge: The Better Way to Connect an Outbuilding

How to choose between fiber and a point-to-point wireless bridge for barns, shops, garages, and detached offices.

July 10, 2026 · Steve Smith

Connecting a detached building is different from extending WiFi to another room. The connection must cross open space and remain stable through weather, interference, and future network growth.

Fiber is the strongest long-term option

Fiber provides high capacity, electrical isolation, and excellent reliability. A dedicated point-to-point bridge can be a strong option when trenching is not practical and there is clear line of sight.
