When a Small Business Should Upgrade to a UniFi Network

Signs that a small business has outgrown consumer routers, unmanaged switches, and one shared WiFi network.

July 10, 2026 · Steve Smith

Many small businesses begin with the router provided by the internet company. That can be enough at first, but the network becomes harder to manage as cameras, phones, point-of-sale systems, staff devices, printers, and guest traffic are added.

Warning signs

Frequent resets, random slowdowns, poor coverage, unknown devices, and a single password shared by staff and visitors are signs that the network needs structure.
