Managed WiFi, cameras, gates, clubhouses, pools, offices, and shared-area connectivity for HOAs and communities.

Shared-area coverage

Clubhouses, pools, offices, parks, and gathering spaces may require guest or resident WiFi.

Outdoor access points are placed around actual use areas.

Gate and camera connectivity

Entry gates, intercoms, cameras, and access devices may need fiber, bridges, or remote PoE.

Long distances and power availability are reviewed.

Separate networks

Staff, residents, guests, cameras, and building devices can use separate networks.

Policies should remain manageable for property staff.

Multi-building design

Fiber or bridges can connect offices, clubhouses, maintenance buildings, and gatehouses.

Each location may need local switching and WiFi.

Documentation and ownership

The HOA should retain clear documentation and administrative ownership of the system.

Vendor and board access can be structured appropriately.

Serving Northern Colorado

Berthoud WiFi is based in Berthoud and serves Loveland, Fort Collins, Longmont, Erie, Boulder, Windsor, Johnstown, Timnath, Mead, Frederick, Firestone, Wellington, Greeley, and nearby communities.
