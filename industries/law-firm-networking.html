Secure staff networks, guest WiFi, conference-room connectivity, cameras, and reliable infrastructure for law offices.

Reliable staff connectivity

Cloud applications, document systems, video meetings, phones, and printers need stable wired and wireless connections.

Conference rooms and private offices require predictable coverage.

Separate guest access

Clients and visitors can receive internet access without joining the private staff network.

Bandwidth and isolation policies can be applied.

Network visibility

Managed gateways and switches provide better insight into connected devices, links, and outages.

Documentation supports future IT service.

Cameras and controlled entry

UniFi Protect and Access can support entrances, offices, file areas, and staff doors.

Door hardware and privacy requirements should be reviewed.

Professional installation

Cabling, racks, labels, UPS protection, and equipment placement are planned around the office.

The finished system remains understandable and supportable.
