Reliable office WiFi, segmented devices, cameras, guest access, and cabling for medical and dental practices.

Separate clinical and guest devices

Staff workstations, clinical devices, printers, cameras, and guests should not all share one unrestricted network.

Segmentation supports cleaner operations and troubleshooting.

Reliable exam-room coverage

Walls, equipment, offices, and patient areas can create inconsistent coverage.

Access points are placed around actual workflows.

Wired critical systems

Fixed workstations, imaging systems, phones, and printers benefit from wired connections where practical.

PoE switching can support phones, cameras, and access points.

Privacy and operational needs

Network design should support the practice’s security and compliance program.

Berthoud WiFi provides technical infrastructure, while the organization remains responsible for its legal and regulatory obligations.

Business continuity

UPS protection, documentation, and remote visibility can reduce disruption.

Internet redundancy may be considered for critical cloud applications.
