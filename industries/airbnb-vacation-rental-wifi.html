Reliable guest WiFi, owner devices, cameras, smart locks, and remote network visibility for short-term rentals.

Separate guest and owner devices

Guests should not share unrestricted access with cameras, locks, thermostats, or management devices.

A separate guest network improves organization.

Simple guest access

WiFi names and passwords should be easy to communicate and change.

Captive portals are possible but may be unnecessary for a single property.

Coverage throughout the rental

Bedrooms, patios, garages, and work areas need reliable service.

Remote workers and streaming guests quickly notice dead zones.

Smart locks and cameras

Locks, doorbells, cameras, thermostats, and noise monitors depend on stable coverage.

Device placement and 2.4 GHz performance matter.

Remote visibility

Managed equipment can help identify outages or disconnected devices before the next guest arrives.

Administrative ownership should remain with the property owner.
