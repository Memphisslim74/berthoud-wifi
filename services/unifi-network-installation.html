Request a QuoteHow Installation Works

A UniFi installation can bring internet routing, switching, WiFi, cameras, access, and device visibility into one manageable platform.

Design before equipment

We determine coverage, port count, PoE needs, internet speed, client density, camera plans, cabling, and future growth before selecting equipment.

Gateways and switching

The gateway and switches are sized around routing, security features, network ports, PoE capacity, uplinks, and expansion.

U6 and U7 access points

We install both families. U6 models remain strong choices for many homes and businesses, while U7 equipment may fit newer clients, 6 GHz use, or future requirements.

Network separation

Staff, guest, camera, IoT, and other device groups can be separated where it improves security and management.

Testing and handoff

The completed system is updated, configured, tested, labeled, and reviewed with the customer.
