UniFi Protect Camera & Security Systems

UniFi Protect cameras, recording, doorbells, and coverage planning.

Professional infrastructure built for the property

Every project is planned around the site, the building materials, existing cabling, device needs, and future growth. Service is available throughout Berthoud, Loveland, Fort Collins, Longmont, Erie, Boulder, and nearby communities.

UniFi Protect provides local recording, remote viewing, and a unified camera platform without relying entirely on consumer cloud subscriptions.

Camera placement

We plan coverage around entrances, driveways, parking areas, shops, barns, gates, and interior spaces while avoiding unnecessary blind spots.

Recording and storage

Recorder and storage recommendations are based on camera count, resolution, retention goals, and expected growth.

Integration

Protect can work alongside UniFi networking and access systems, making it easier to manage cameras, devices, and events in one environment.

Residential and business use

We install systems for homes, offices, shops, rural properties, and other Northern Colorado locations.

Typical project scope

  • Interior and exterior cameras
  • UniFi Protect recording
  • Doorbell cameras
  • Driveway, shop, and barn coverage
