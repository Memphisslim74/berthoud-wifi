A useful camera system needs the right views, dependable recording, adequate storage, and straightforward access when an event occurs.

Coverage before camera count

We begin with entrances, driveways, parking areas, walkways, work areas, shops, barns, gates, and other important zones.

Choose the camera for the view

Turret, dome, bullet, panoramic, AI, doorbell, and specialty cameras serve different purposes. Selection depends on distance, lighting, mounting, identification needs, and weather exposure.

Recording and retention

Recorder and storage recommendations are based on camera count, resolution, expected retention, and future expansion.

Network and power planning

PoE switching, cable routes, surge protection, recorder placement, and bandwidth are part of the camera design.

Remote viewing and alerts

The system is configured for appropriate viewing, notifications, user access, and day-to-day management.
