Camera installation begins with the view and the event you need to capture—not with placing the same camera model everywhere.

Define important views

Entrances, driveways, parking, gates, work areas, cash-handling areas, shops, and barns may require different coverage.

Choose appropriate cameras

Resolution, lens, night performance, mounting, weather exposure, identification distance, and AI features influence selection.

Cabling and PoE

Reliable camera systems depend on proper cable routes, switching capacity, surge considerations, and recorder placement.

Storage and retention

Storage is planned around camera count, resolution, frame rate, and how many days of footage are needed.

Remote viewing

Users and alerts are configured so the system is useful without creating unnecessary notification noise.
