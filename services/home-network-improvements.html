Home Internet & WiFi Improvements

Home WiFi modernization, UniFi access points, wired backhaul, and better coverage.

Professional infrastructure built for the property

Every project is planned around the site, the building materials, existing cabling, device needs, and future growth. Service is available throughout Berthoud, Loveland, Fort Collins, Longmont, Erie, Boulder, and nearby communities.

A reliable home network should support work, streaming, gaming, cameras, smart-home devices, guests, and mobile devices without constant resets or dead zones.

Why home WiFi struggles

Coverage problems are often caused by access points being placed at one end of the house, thick walls, multiple floors, basements, garages, metal ductwork, stone, brick, and too many devices sharing one weak radio. Adding extenders can make roaming and speed worse because every repeated hop consumes capacity.

How we improve it

We evaluate the internet handoff, current router, cabling options, floor plan, and the areas where devices are actually used. Depending on the home, we may recommend UniFi U6 or U7 access points, wired backhaul, a new gateway, managed switching, separate guest and IoT networks, and outdoor coverage.

U6 and U7 recommendations

We do not recommend WiFi 7 just because it is newer. Many homes are better served by proven U6 models, including options with stronger 5 GHz radio capability. We match equipment to coverage, client density, internet speed, and budget rather than marketing labels.

Common projects

Typical projects include improving upstairs coverage, extending service to a basement or garage, stabilizing work-from-home calls, adding camera coverage, supporting smart-home devices, and replacing unstable consumer mesh systems.

Typical project scope

  • Whole-home WiFi planning
  • Basement, garage, and patio coverage
  • Wired access points and backhaul
  • Streaming, gaming, and work-from-home stability
