Outdoor WiFi Installation

Reliable connectivity for patios, yards, pools, work areas, restaurants, shops, and rural outdoor spaces.

Request a QuoteHow Installation Works

Outdoor coverage should be created with outdoor-rated equipment placed for the area—not by hoping an indoor access point reaches through an exterior wall.

Coverage around the use area

We plan around patios, pools, yards, work areas, seating, event spaces, parking areas, and paths where devices are actually used.

Weather-rated equipment

Outdoor access points are selected for exposure, mounting, coverage pattern, client load, and available backhaul.

Wired or wireless backhaul

Ethernet is preferred when practical. Fiber or a dedicated wireless link may be appropriate for distant areas or separate structures.

Commercial outdoor spaces

Restaurants, offices, venues, shops, and other businesses may need separate guest access, staff coverage, cameras, or point-of-sale connectivity outdoors.

Testing and adjustment

Outdoor performance is tested from representative locations rather than only beside the access point.
