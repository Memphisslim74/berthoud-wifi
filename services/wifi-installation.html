Professional WiFi Installation

Planned WiFi coverage for homes, businesses, outdoor spaces, and multi-building properties.

Get a quoteHow Installation Works

Professional WiFi installation is more than placing another router. Coverage, backhaul, channels, mounting, switching, and the property itself all matter.

Coverage goals

We identify the rooms, floors, work areas, outdoor spaces, and buildings where reliable service is required.

Access-point placement

Ceiling, wall, indoor, outdoor, and specialty access points have different coverage patterns. Placement is chosen around construction and device use.

Wired backhaul

Ethernet or fiber backhaul gives access points a dependable connection and avoids the capacity loss associated with repeated wireless hops.

Appropriate equipment

U6 and U7 models are selected based on coverage, client density, radio capability, internet speed, and cost.

Testing the result

Coverage and connectivity are tested in representative areas before the project is complete.
