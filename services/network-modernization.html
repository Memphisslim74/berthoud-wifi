Internet & Network Modernization

Replace aging routers, unmanaged switches, unstable mesh systems, and outdated equipment.

Professional infrastructure built for the property

Every project is planned around the site, the building materials, existing cabling, device needs, and future growth. Service is available throughout Berthoud, Loveland, Fort Collins, Longmont, Erie, Boulder, and nearby communities.

Network modernization replaces aging routers, unmanaged switches, weak mesh systems, tangled cabling, and undocumented equipment with a cleaner platform.

What modernization includes

We inventory the existing network, identify weak links, remove unnecessary equipment, redesign switching and WiFi, improve cable organization, and document the finished system.

Why it matters

Older equipment often causes random slowdowns, outages, poor visibility, and difficult troubleshooting. Modernization improves reliability and creates a foundation for cameras, access control, faster internet, and future expansion.

Not every device must be replaced

We reuse appropriate equipment where it makes sense. The goal is a stable, supportable system—not replacing hardware simply because something newer exists.

Typical outcomes

Customers gain better coverage, cleaner racks, fewer resets, clearer device visibility, improved guest access, and a system that can be managed remotely.

Typical project scope

  • Network inventory and cleanup
  • Gateway replacement
  • Managed switching
  • WiFi redesign
  • Rack and cable organization
