Network & WiFi Troubleshooting

Diagnosis for slow WiFi, dead zones, dropped connections, unstable cameras, and unreliable networks.

Get a quoteHow Installation Works

Network problems are rarely solved well by guessing. We isolate whether the issue involves the internet service, gateway, switching, cabling, WiFi, interference, configuration, or client devices.

Start with the symptoms

We review where failures occur, which devices are affected, how often the issue happens, and what changed before the problem began.

Test the wired path

Internet handoff, gateway performance, switch links, cabling, PoE, and device errors are checked before blaming WiFi.

Evaluate wireless coverage

Access-point placement, channel use, signal strength, interference, roaming, and client density are reviewed.

Repair or replace

Some networks need tuning, a cable repair, or better placement. Others have outgrown consumer equipment or an aging design.

Provide a clear next step

You receive an explanation of the likely cause and practical options for correcting it.
