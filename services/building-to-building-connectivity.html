UniFi Services

Building-to-Building Connectivity

Wireless bridge and fiber solutions for detached garages, barns, shops, offices, and gates.

Professional infrastructure built for the property

Every project is planned around the site, the building materials, existing cabling, device needs, and future growth. Service is available throughout Berthoud, Loveland, Fort Collins, Longmont, Erie, Boulder, and nearby communities.

Detached buildings require a dedicated connection strategy. Consumer extenders are rarely the right answer for barns, shops, garages, gates, and offices.

Wireless bridges

A point-to-point bridge can provide a strong link when there is a clear line of sight and trenching is impractical.

Fiber links

Fiber is the preferred long-term choice when conduit or trenching is available and the property needs maximum reliability.

The remote building still needs a network

Once connectivity reaches the building, we may install switching, WiFi, cameras, or wired connections inside the structure.

Property-specific design

Distance, terrain, mounting height, line of sight, weather exposure, and future needs all influence the final design.

Typical project scope

  • Point-to-point bridges
  • Fiber links
  • Detached garage and workshop networks
  • Barn and gate connectivity
