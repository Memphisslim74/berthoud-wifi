Building-to-Building Connectivity

Dedicated wireless bridge and fiber solutions for barns, shops, garages, gates, and detached offices.

Get a quoteHow Installation Works

A detached building needs a dedicated network connection. A consumer extender in a window is rarely a dependable long-term solution.

Wireless bridge options

Point-to-point bridges can connect structures without trenching when mounting locations and line of sight are suitable.

When fiber is the better choice

Fiber is ideal where conduit or trenching is practical, maximum reliability is required, or electrical isolation is important.

Designing the remote building

The remote structure may need its own switch, access points, cameras, workstations, phones, or equipment connections.

Site-specific factors

Distance, terrain, trees, rooflines, mounting height, power, weather exposure, and future growth all influence the final recommendation.

Testing the finished link

We verify link quality, throughput, switching, WiFi coverage, and connected equipment before handoff.
