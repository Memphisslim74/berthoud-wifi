UniFi Services

Small Business Network Infrastructure

UniFi gateways, switching, WiFi, security, and guest networks for small businesses.

Professional infrastructure built for the property

Every project is planned around the site, the building materials, existing cabling, device needs, and future growth. Service is available throughout Berthoud, Loveland, Fort Collins, Longmont, Erie, Boulder, and nearby communities.

Small businesses need dependable infrastructure for staff, guests, point-of-sale systems, phones, printers, cameras, and cloud applications.

A cleaner business network

We replace consumer-grade routers and unmanaged switches with a documented UniFi platform that provides visibility, segmentation, and room to grow.

Segmentation and security

Staff, guests, cameras, point-of-sale systems, and smart devices can be separated into appropriate networks. This improves troubleshooting and reduces unnecessary exposure between devices.

Infrastructure that scales

Projects can include gateways, managed PoE switching, access points, camera systems, access control, rack organization, UPS protection, cabling, and remote monitoring.

Who we serve

Berthoud WiFi supports offices, shops, churches, studios, retail locations, restaurants, nonprofits, and other small businesses across Northern Colorado.

Typical project scope

  • Business gateways and firewalls
  • PoE switching and access points
  • Secure staff and guest networks
  • Point-of-sale and device segmentation
