Small Business Network Infrastructure

Reliable UniFi networking, WiFi, switching, cameras, and secure guest access for Northern Colorado businesses.

Request a QuoteHow Installation Works

Small businesses rely on their network for far more than internet access. Staff devices, guest WiFi, phones, printers, point-of-sale systems, cameras, cloud applications, and building systems all compete for the same infrastructure.

A practical business network

We design systems that are dependable, understandable, and appropriately sized for the business. That can include a UniFi gateway, managed PoE switching, U6 or U7 access points, separate staff and guest networks, camera infrastructure, and clean rack or wall-mounted equipment.

Separate critical systems

Staff traffic, guest access, cameras, point-of-sale devices, printers, and IoT equipment should not all share one open network. Appropriate segmentation makes the environment easier to troubleshoot and helps limit unnecessary access between devices.

WiFi designed for the space

Coverage is planned around walls, shelving, offices, customer areas, outdoor seating, warehouses, and device density. We may recommend proven U6 models or newer U7 equipment depending on client needs, cabling, coverage goals, and budget.

Clean switching and cabling

Managed switches, PoE capacity, patch panels, rack organization, labeling, and UPS protection make the network easier to maintain. Existing cabling and equipment can be reused when it remains appropriate.

Cameras and access

UniFi Protect cameras and UniFi Access can be included in the same overall infrastructure plan. Camera placement, storage, door hardware, cabling, and network capacity should be considered together.

Ongoing support

After installation, we test the system, document the important settings, explain day-to-day use, and provide a clear path for future expansion or support.
