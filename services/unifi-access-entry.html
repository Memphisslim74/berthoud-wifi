Access control works best when door hardware, power, cabling, network connectivity, cameras, and user workflows are planned as one system.

Controlled entry

Readers and access hubs can manage authorized entry and provide visibility into door events.

Credentials and users

Employees or approved users can receive credentials appropriate to their role, schedule, or location.

Intercom and visitor workflows

Intercom systems can support visitor communication, remote response, and controlled entry.

Door and life-safety considerations

Existing locks, strikes, request-to-exit devices, door position sensors, and local requirements must be reviewed as part of the project.

Integration with cameras and networking

Access events, network infrastructure, and nearby camera coverage can be planned together for a more complete installation.
