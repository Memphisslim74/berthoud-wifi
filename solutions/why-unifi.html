Why Berthoud WiFi Uses UniFi

Why UniFi is a strong platform for home, small-business, camera, access-control, and multi-building network installations.

Get a quoteInstallation Process

UniFi combines gateways, switches, WiFi, cameras, access control, and management in one ecosystem. It offers professional visibility and scalability without requiring traditional enterprise licensing for every feature.

Unified platformNetworking, Protect cameras, Access, switching, and WiFi.
Deployment rangeHomes, small businesses, churches, shops, rural properties, and offices.
Hardware choiceU6 and U7 access points, multiple gateways, switches, cameras, and bridges.
Important caveatThe platform still requires proper design, cabling, configuration, and support.

Central management

UniFi provides a single environment for viewing gateways, switches, access points, clients, cameras, and access devices. This reduces the need to manage unrelated consumer applications.

Central visibility helps identify disconnected devices, weak links, PoE status, and network changes.

Scalable hardware choices

The product family includes compact gateways, rack-mounted consoles, small and large switches, indoor and outdoor access points, cameras, recorders, bridges, and door-access equipment.

The challenge is selecting equipment based on the property rather than assuming the most expensive or newest model is best.

U6 and U7 flexibility

UniFi U6 models remain strong options for many installations, including environments that benefit from their radio configuration, compatibility, and value. U7 products may fit newer clients, 6 GHz needs, multi-gig networks, or future planning.

Berthoud WiFi installs both generations and explains the tradeoffs.

Home and small-business fit

UniFi offers more visibility and control than typical consumer mesh systems while remaining approachable for homes and small organizations.

Features such as multiple networks, guest access, managed switching, PoE, camera integration, and remote management provide room to grow.

Professional installation still matters

Good hardware cannot compensate for poor access-point placement, weak cabling, undersized PoE, bad camera views, or an unplanned rack. The platform performs best when the entire system is designed together.

We focus on coverage, backhaul, switching, power, segmentation, documentation, and testing.

Frequently asked questions

Is UniFi only for businesses?

No. UniFi works well in homes, rural properties, and small businesses when the system is sized appropriately.

Does UniFi require monthly licensing?

Many core UniFi management features do not use the traditional per-device licensing model common in enterprise networking. Specific third-party services or optional offerings may have separate costs.

Do you install both U6 and U7 access points?

Yes. We recommend the model that fits the environment.

Can UniFi manage cameras and door access?

Yes. UniFi Protect and UniFi Access can be integrated with the network platform.
