Berthoud WiFi provides professional network installation for Superior homes, offices, shops, churches, restaurants, rural properties, and multi-building sites.

Home WiFi and dead-zone correction

Residential projects can include UniFi U6 or U7 access points, wired backhaul, guest and IoT networks, home-office connectivity, outdoor WiFi, and camera systems.

Business network infrastructure
