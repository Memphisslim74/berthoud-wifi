Home WiFi installation in Frederick

Residential projects can include whole-home WiFi, U6 or U7 access points, wired backhaul, garage and patio coverage, camera systems, smart-home networking, and replacement of unstable mesh systems.

Business network infrastructure

Business installations can include gateways, managed PoE switching, staff and guest networks, point-of-sale connectivity, cameras, access control, racks, UPS protection, and structured cabling.

Rural and multi-building connectivity

Properties with shops, barns, detached garages, gates, or offices may use fiber, point-to-point bridges, outdoor access points, remote switching, and UniFi Protect cameras.

Why choose Berthoud WiFi
